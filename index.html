Sadie Elizabeth Sink[2] (born April 16, 2002) is an American actress. After working on Broadway as the titular lead in Annie, Sink received her breakthrough roles as Max Mayfield in the science fiction television series Stranger Things and Ziggy Berman in horror film trilogy Fear Street.
She played the lead role in Taylor Swift's romantic drama All Too Well: The Short Film (2021) and is set to star in the psychological drama film The Whale (2022). Her accolades include nominations for two Screen Actors Guild Awards and two MTV Movie & TV Awards.
